About Tiny Bear

We are more than just a private home daycare; it is a nurturing environment designed to meet the needs of your entire family. Our goal is to create a happy place for your little ones to play, discover, and learn while growing up.

Every color, every corner, every detail of our bear den was carefully crafted with your child's best interests in mind. So tiny, your baby is not yet ready to leave the nest, so our aim is to extend the comfort and security of your home to our daycare, so your child can feel safe, loved, and cared for at all times.

At Tiny Bear, we recognize the importance of play-based learning and exploration, especially when it comes to incorporating elements of nature. We believe that play is a crucial part of the learning process, helping to facilitate creativity, autonomy, and overall growth.

Each child who becomes a part of the Tiny Bear family is unique, with their own individual personality, interests, and feelings. We understand that these traits are constantly evolving and changing, and we strive to create learning moments that reflect each child's natural curiosities and inclinations.

Our approach to learning is flexible, supportive, and natural. We believe that true learning occurs when a child is not forced or disciplined, but rather encouraged in an open and creative environment. Our aim is to foster and encourage the individuality of every child who walks through our doors.


Mission

Our mission at Tiny Bear is to provide a solid foundation for your child's future through education and love. We believe that the experiences a child has during their early years can have a powerful impact on their future. That's why we're committed to creating a nurturing environment that fosters growth, development, and a love for learning.

Vision and Values

Our vision at Tiny Bear is to be the first and only choice for discerning, nurturing parents seeking a high-quality early child development and preschool. We are dedicated to providing the highest level of care and education to help your child reach their full potential.

Our goal at Tiny Bear, we are dedicated to promoting excellence in the field of early childhood education and fostering the growth and development of every child in our care. We believe that providing a nurturing environment and high-quality educational opportunities can have a profound impact on a child's future success and well-being.

We are made up of passionate professionals who are committed to creating a supportive and engaging learning environment. Whether your child is just starting their educational journey or is preparing to transition to primary school, we are here to support their growth and development every step of the way.

Additionally, we strive to be a valuable resource and partner for parents in search of high-quality child care. We understand that finding the right child care provider can be a challenging task, and we're here to help make the process easier.

To achieve this vision, we prioritize the ongoing professional development. We accomplish this through our commitment to annual trainings that go beyond the requirements of most child care programs. By maintaining the highest standards of teaching and learning, we are confident that Tiny Bear will be the go-to destination for families seeking exceptional early childhood education.

About Me

Working with children is a great pleasure for me, specially when I notice their rapid progress and growth. I was fortunate enough to begin my career in Toronto as a babysitter, during my studying time at college, where my passion for working with children grew stronger each day. Eventually, after a brainstorm with my family, I decided to turn my passion into a full-time career.

I have always enjoyed planning various activities to help children learn and develop, while encouraging their independence. However, I also love being there to support and assist them whenever they need it.

I am certified in CPR and in First Aid, and I have been working with children for many years. My love for children, patience and understanding their special needs will help me to provide a safe, nurturing, and fun environment for your child and also, become their best friend.I believe in building strong relationships with each child and their family.

We are excited to learn more about your little one and your family, so that we can tailor our approach to meet your unique needs and goals. Thank you for considering us as your child's partner in learning and growth.
