Tiny Bear Program

At Tiny Bear, we believe in giving children the tools they need to thrive in both school and life. Through a balance of play-based learning and educational activities, we provide infants, toddlers and preschoolers children with the skills they need to succeed.

Our daily routine strikes a balance between play and learning activities. Children will participate in both individual and group activities, allowing them to learn at their own pace while also developing social skills.

While each day is unique and tailored to the children's needs and interests, we generally follow a schedule that includes a variety of engaging activities throughout the day. Following our general schedule below:
